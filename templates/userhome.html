Online Book Exchange

Online Book Exchange provides a platform for users to register, log in, and interact with others to borrow or exchange books. By providing a user-friendly interface and efficient book management functionalities, the Online Book Exchange aims to enhance the accessibility and sharing of books within a community promoting the reuse and sharing of resources. We also contributes to the development of sustainable and eco-friendly practices by encouraging book exchanges instead of purchasing new ones.
